# SteadyUs Coach Call Notes Template

**Version date:** 2026-05-17  
**Audience:** Internal delivery source

This template is an internal working document for delivering the **Couples Budget Reset**. It is intended to keep notes consistent across Session 1 and Session 2 while preserving the calm, practical, non-judgemental tone of the offer. The focus is on clarity, priorities, and realistic next steps rather than exhaustive financial data capture.

## Scope reminder

This session provides budgeting support and financial organisation only. It does not include regulated financial, investment, pension, tax, mortgage-product, or legal advice. It is also not therapy, crisis support, or debt counselling. If any such issues arise, note them briefly and redirect the conversation back to what can be handled within scope.

### Session 1 summary paragraph

Write a short paragraph summarising what is actually going on beneath the surface. Focus on the practical pattern, not on blame.

> Example frame: “The main issue is not simply overspending or lack of information. The bigger pattern appears to be that the couple feels unclear about priorities, with one partner carrying more of the day-to-day tracking and both avoiding decisions when money feels emotionally loaded.”

## Internal reminder

The aim of these notes is not to create a formal report. The aim is to capture enough detail to support a calm, useful, practical client experience. If the notes become too technical or too long, reduce them back to the essentials: what is going on, what matters most, what the couple agreed, and what happens next.
