# Welcome to SteadyUs

**Version date:** 2026-05-17  
**Audience:** Client delivery

Thank you for choosing **SteadyUs**.

This pack is here to help you feel clearer about what happens next. The aim of SteadyUs is to help couples make money feel calmer, clearer, and easier to talk about. It is designed for couples who are doing reasonably well on paper but still feel friction, confusion, stress, or a lack of shared direction around money.

Your pack includes a small set of practical documents. The sales decks explain the service at a high level. The client delivery deck and client session guide help you understand the structure of the sessions and what to expect before, during, and after them. Everything in this client-safe pack is intended to be suitable for external sharing and personal reference.

Across the process, the focus is not on blame or judgement. The purpose is to create a clearer shared picture, reduce recurring money tension, and leave you with a more realistic way of making decisions together. The sessions are structured, but they are also practical and supportive.

Before Session 1, it is helpful to come ready to talk honestly about the current picture. Between sessions, you may be asked to reflect on a few points or gather a small amount of information. By the end of the process, the goal is that you have more clarity, more shared language, and a clearer next-step plan.

If you are ever unsure which file to open first, start with `START_HERE_WHICH_FILE_TO_USE.md`. If you want the shortest overview, open the at-a-glance sales summary. If you want to understand the client journey itself, open the client session guide.

I hope this pack helps you feel more settled and more prepared for the work ahead.
