# Pairwise Backup Naming Shortlist

**Date:** 2026-04-09  
**Prepared by:** Manus AI

## Naming objective

The fallback name should preserve the product thesis of **shared financial decisions for couples** without sounding clinical, mathematical, or overly fintech-heavy. It should feel calm, collaborative, and trustworthy in a UK consumer context, while also being distinct enough to reduce the crowding and confusion risk currently attached to **Pairwise**.

## Shortlist

| Candidate name | Why it fits the product | Risk posture compared with Pairwise | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Nestwise** | Suggests building a home, a shared base, and wiser joint decisions. | Lower, because it is more distinctive and less generic than Pairwise. | Strong if the roadmap keeps a home-goal angle. |
| **Twofold Money** | Conveys two people making one coordinated financial plan. | Lower, because it is less likely to collide with existing consumer apps. | Slightly more abstract, but brandable. |
| **Together Ledger** | Signals partnership and money management in plain English. | Lower, because it is descriptive but still distinctive in combination. | Feels practical and trustworthy. |
| **Common Ground Money** | Suggests alignment, compromise, and finding a shared path. | Lower, because it is phrase-based and more ownable than Pairwise. | Good for coaching-led positioning. |
| **Home & Balance** | Works well if the product focuses on trade-offs between stability, debt, and home goals. | Lower, though more descriptive. | Friendly consumer tone. |
| **Shared Step** | Suggests a next move taken together rather than a static budgeting tool. | Lower, because it is more emotive and less generic. | Strong fit with your current promise. |
| **Side by Sum** | Memorable and slightly playful while keeping a money cue. | Lower to medium, depending on search results. | More creative; may need user testing. |
| **Aligned Money** | Clearly communicates the product outcome: financial alignment between partners. | Lower, but a little more category-descriptive. | Clean and direct. |

## Strongest directions

The strongest options at this stage are **Nestwise**, **Shared Step**, and **Common Ground Money**.

These work best because they preserve the emotional core of the product. The product is not really about spreadsheets or budgeting mechanics alone. It is about helping two people agree on a sensible next move without tension or confusion. The best replacement name should therefore sound like **coordination and calm progress**, not like a technical decision model.

## Recommendation by strategic angle

| Strategic angle | Best-fit name |
|---|---|
| Home-focused UK couples money planning | **Nestwise** |
| Broad shared-decision product for couples | **Shared Step** |
| Coaching-led or guidance-led brand | **Common Ground Money** |
| Straightforward money-management framing | **Aligned Money** |

## Launch contingency recommendation

The pragmatic launch approach is to treat **Pairwise** as the internal working name until the official UK IPO check is completed, while quietly preparing one fallback name for fast substitution in public-facing materials.

My recommendation is to keep **one primary backup** and **one reserve backup**:
