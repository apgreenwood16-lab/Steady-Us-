Session 2

Shared action plan

The second session turns a clearer money picture into decisions the couple can actually use: what matters now, what can wait, and what to do next together.
This is where the conversation becomes practical. The goal is not another abstract discussion, but a realistic plan that feels shared, manageable, and clear enough to follow after the call.
01
Agree realistic priorities
The couple narrows the conversation down to the few priorities that matter most right now, instead of trying to solve everything at once.
02
Turn intentions into habits
The discussion becomes practical routines, simple decisions, and manageable habits that fit real schedules and real pressure.
03
Define the next actions clearly
By the end, both partners know what happens next, what to keep focused on, and how to avoid slipping back into another vague money loop.
What they leave with
A shared written plan, practical next steps, and clearer agreement on how to move forward without one partner carrying all the mental load.
