Session 1

Money clarity starts with the real picture

The first session is designed to slow things down, make the current situation visible, and help both partners understand where the pressure is really coming from.
01
Map the current money picture
Income, spending, savings and obligations are shown together for a clear starting point.
02
Surface the pressure points
We pinpoint where money feels stretched, avoided, or emotionally loaded.
03
Identify the main sources of friction
Patterns that create recurring tension are highlighted to guide next steps.
What this session gives them

A shared starting point

Both partners can see the same financial picture clearly, which reduces confusion and stops the conversation from scattering.

Less hidden pressure

Unspoken worries, recurring pinch points, and low-grade overwhelm are brought into the open in a calmer, more useful way.

Clearer next-session focus

By the end of session one, it is easier to know what needs attention first and what the shared action plan should solve next.

This first conversation is not about solving everything at once. It is about making the situation understandable enough to move forward together.
