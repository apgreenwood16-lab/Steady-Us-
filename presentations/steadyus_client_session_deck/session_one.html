Session 1

Session 1 brings clarity

The first session is designed to slow things down and make the current money picture easier to understand together. It helps both of you see what is really going on before trying to fix everything at once.
01

See the full picture

We bring income, spending, savings, obligations, and current pressures into one shared view so the conversation feels clearer and less scattered.

02

Name what feels heavy

We look at the areas that feel tense, unclear, or repeatedly avoided, so the main pressure points become visible instead of vague.

03

Leave with clearer focus

By the end of the session, you should have a stronger sense of what is causing friction and what needs practical attention next.

Main outcome

You leave Session 1 with a shared starting point: a clearer picture of where things stand now, what feels most pressured, and which issues are worth focusing on in Session 2.

Helpful reminder

This session is about understanding the situation properly. It is not about solving every issue immediately or arriving with a perfect system already in place.
