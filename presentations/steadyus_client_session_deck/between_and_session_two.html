Between sessions and Session 2

Light follow-through, then a shared plan

Between the sessions, the aim is to keep momentum going without turning the process into heavy homework. Then Session 2 uses that clearer picture to create practical priorities and next steps you can actually use.
Between sessions stays simple
You may receive a few short prompts or follow-up questions. The goal is to keep the important issues visible, not to ask you to build a complete system on your own.
Prompt 1
Keep it light Brief reflection is enough. You do not need to prepare a perfect spreadsheet or detailed budget before the next call.
Prompt 2
Notice what matters Pay attention to what still feels urgent, unclear, or most important now that the first conversation has slowed things down.
Prompt 3
Stay realistic The purpose is practical progress, not a perfect reset overnight. Session 2 will help turn that reflection into clearer decisions.
Session 2 turns clarity into action
The second session helps you decide what matters most first, what can wait, and how to turn your intentions into a shared plan that feels manageable in real life.
Outcome 1
Agree the priorities You identify the few issues that matter most instead of trying to fix everything at once.
Outcome 2
Build a usable plan You translate those priorities into actions, routines, and habits that fit ordinary life rather than ideal conditions.
Outcome 3
Clarify what happens next You leave knowing who is doing what, what to focus on first, and what a more organised money rhythm can look like together.
By the end of Session 2, the goal is not just better understanding — it is a calmer, clearer way to move forward together.
