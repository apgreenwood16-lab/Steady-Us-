What this package is for

A calmer way to sort money together

The Couples Budget Reset is designed to help you understand your money situation together, reduce confusion, and leave with a clearer shared plan. It focuses on budgeting support and financial organisation, not regulated advice.

What you can expect

01
A calmer conversationSessions reduce pressure and help you both see the same picture more clearly.
02
Practical supportFocus is on getting organised together, not creating a perfect system overnight.
03
Shared prioritiesYou leave with clear next steps, ownership, and a usable plan.
04
A written summaryAfter sessions you receive a concise recap of priorities, decisions, and focus points.

What this is not

01
Not regulated financial adviceThis does not include investment, pension, tax, or mortgage-product recommendations.
02
Not debt or legal supportSpecialist debt, dispute, or legal issues need separate expert help.
03
Not therapySessions focus on money organisation and next steps, not therapeutic support.
04
Not about judgementYou do not need to arrive fully organised—the point is to create clarity from where you are now.
Best fit: couples who are doing reasonably well on paper but still feel stretched, disorganised, or stuck when it comes to money.
